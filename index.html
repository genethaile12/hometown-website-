About Asmara

Asmara is the capital city of Eritrea, known for its art deco architecture and vibrant culture. It’s a UNESCO World Heritage Site!

The Weather

Asmara enjoys mild weather year-round, with sunny days and cool evenings, making it a delightful place to live and visit.

Famous Landmark

The Fiat Tagliero Building is an iconic art deco structure that symbolizes Asmara’s unique charm and rich heritage.
